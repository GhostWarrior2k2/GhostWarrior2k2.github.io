Taskify

A Simple Task Management App Taskify is a basic task management application that allows you to create boards, cards, and tasks. You can add tasks with due times, prioritize them, and track your progress.

Key Features:

  • Board and card management: Create boards and cards to organize your tasks.
  • Task management: Add tasks with due times and prioritize them.
